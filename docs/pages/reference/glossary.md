# Glossary

## Actuator basis

The fixed set of device-owned primitive control dimensions. It is external and bounded. The model learns to use it; the basis does not grow from input history.

## Actuator trace

A prepared internal trace over actuator primitives. It is not rendered text. It may be partial, malformed, competing, or unfinished.

## Articulation buffer

The internal preparation surface where actuator traces accumulate before release.

## Reafference

The sensed consequence of the model's own action returning through the receptor path.

## Renderer

A device/body surface that turns actuator trace into external witness. It is not an author and must not repair output.

## UTE

Receptor-side transduction. World to model through bounded sensing.

## UTD

Effector-side transduction. Model to world through bounded actuation.

## Witness

A rendered external trace of internal activity. It is measurable but is not identical to internal state.

## Void walkers

Runtime/topology actors retained as part of the internal event-map/void-walker system. They are not UI visualization agents.

## SIE

A retained runtime/system family with multiple variants. Do not collapse or delete variants without tracing their distinct use and intended role.
